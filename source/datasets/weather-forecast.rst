.. weather forecast

.. grid::

   .. grid-item-card::

      This dataset contains weather forecast for South Tyrol. Updated
      daily at 07:00 AM and 11:00 AM (Sundays only at 10 AM), the
      bulletin contains the following forecasts:

      * current and next day weather, and five days evolution in the
        whole South Tyrol
      * for current and next day in mountain
      * current weather and next two days evolution for each South
        Tyrolean districts.

        All data is requested live through the Weather service of the
        Province of Bolzano-Bozen (South Tyrol). Data is available in
        English, German, and Italian language.
