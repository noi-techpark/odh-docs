   -----
      
   This datasets contains aggregated data collected by sensors drowned
   in asphalt, but separated for each lane of a street. For people
   that travel on Italian streets, these sensors are those large
   rectangle boxes engraved in asphalt that you can seen also on
   streets in the cities.
