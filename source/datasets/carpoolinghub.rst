.. carpoolinghub

.. grid::

   .. grid-item-card::
      :columns: 6

      This dataset contains all data of the carpooling service of the
      Burggrafenamt/Burgraviato region in South Tyrol, including
      carpooling users, car availability, and locations with accessible
      destinations.

      .. note:: Only users that have explicitly expressed their consent
         to privacy are included, no sensitive data is stored or
         exchanged.
