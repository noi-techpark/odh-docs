.. activity

.. panels::

   -----

   This dataset contains data about activities from LTS, including
   local tours, hiking, running & fitness, cross-country skiing,
   alpine climbing, e-bike, downhill, nordic walking, and many
   more. This dataset offers LTS categorization and enhanced
   filtering.
